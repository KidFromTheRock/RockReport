Build the Player: College-Ready Means More Than Game-Ready

The path to college sports is bigger than Friday night.

Training, nutrition, recovery, grades, eligibility, recruiting windows, transfer rules and roster management all shape the opportunity in front of a student-athlete.

One example: Division I has adopted an age-based eligibility model for prospects initially enrolling full time in college in fall 2027 or later. Families will need to understand what the five-year window means for development, redshirting, injuries and long-term planning.
