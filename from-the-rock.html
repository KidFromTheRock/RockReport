From the Rock: The Pipeline Is Real

Kid From The Rock is built to make the pipeline easier to see.

That means athletes from American Samoa, players of Polynesian descent across the mainland, former players, coaches, families and the people helping move the next generation forward.

As the rankings site comes together, this section will become the place to track who is rising, who is committing, who is transferring, who is coaching and who is carrying the culture into new spaces.

We’ll spotlight:

  • American Samoa athletes.
  • Polynesian athletes across the mainland.
  • Area-code and regional standouts.
  • High school, college and pro stories.
  • Coaches, families and community leaders.

The point is simple: celebrate the ones walking the path and make it easier for the next one to follow.
